Dogecoin

 Dogecoin (DOGE) is a cryptocurrency created by Billy Markus and Jackson Palmer. They made this cryptocurrency as a joke, making fun of cryptocurrencies at the time. It is the first "meme coin", and the first "dog coin". Despite its nature, some consider it an investment prospect. Dogecoin features the face of Kabosu from the "doge" meme as its logo and namesake. It was introduced on December 6, 2013, and quickly developed its own online community, reaching a peak capitalization of over US$85 billion on May 5, 2021 1 DOGE = 0.41 USD

Litecoin

 Litecoin (LTC) is a decentralized cryptocurrency and open-source software project released under the MIT/X11 license. Inspired by Bitcoin, Litecoin was one of the the earliest altcoins, starting in October 2011. Litecoin main chain shares a slightly modified Bitcoin codebase. Due to the similarities to Bitcoin, Litecoin has historically been referred to as the "silver to Bitcoin's gold." 1 LTC = 94.16 USD
